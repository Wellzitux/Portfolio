ABOUT GAMES TECHNICAL
ABOUT My name is Welliton Souza. I'm a passionate game developer, working professionaly with games for 3 years with focus on Technical Art , UI/UX and programming skills. I develop a lot of projects in game jams, where I can test my skills and study more about Game Design and Art, undestanding the importance and how to communicate with all areas in game development. I truly believe that video games are the most impactful media to tell a story that can make people grow and experience different perspectives. That's why i look for making games that can talk to people, entertaining and making the diference.
GAMES
Cobra Kai 2: Dojo's Rising Released for PS4/PS5, XBOX One/Series, Steam and Nintendo Switch, i worked as a Technical Artist and later mainly as the UI Technical Lead. There were a lot of challenges around the implementations of the UI and specific features, mainly:
- Organize the hierarchy of all UI elements in the best optimized way to avoid a high processing cost;
- Implement all animations designed by the UI Team, from tween script based animations to standard clips;
- Implement systems to drag and drop tween and UI offscreen indicators components to ease implement them;
The Walking Dead: Destinies
Released for PS4/PS5, XBOX One/Series, Steam and Nintendo Switch, as the UI Coordinador and Technical art, I worked closely to the programming team to implement all UI and HUDs of the game. Across all challenges, I can highlight the d evelopment of UI Shaders on HLSL to specificaly mask UI Elements and the creation of a the UI/UX Pipeline.
Stand and Fight Release for Android, Stand and Fight won the QuickPlay 2022 jam, hosted by Latinx in Gaming, and later released as a full version game after 6 months of development. I worked as a UI/UX Designer, programmer, technical artist and game designer with a small team, and it was very challenging to enhance a bigger version of a jam game. Developing UI VFX and Shaders and all UI systems that communicate with the gameplay components were my challenges of this project.
