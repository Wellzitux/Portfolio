ABOUT My name is Welliton Souza. I'm a passionate game developer, working professionaly with games for the last 3 years, focusing on Technical Art , UI/UX and programming skills. I develop a lot of projects in game jams, where I was able to enhance my skills and study more about Game Design and 2D Art . These projects made me understand the importance and how to communicate with all areas of game development. I truly believe that video games are the most impactful media to tell a story where people can grow and experience moments from different perspectives. That's why I look for making games that can talk to, entertain and make the diference in people life.
